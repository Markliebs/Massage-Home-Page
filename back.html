Living in pain is not only difficult physically but also mentally. Your entire quality of life changes when your back hurts. Let me help you live a life with less pain.

As someone who has suffered from herniated disc's, bulging disc's, sciatica and piriformis syndrome, I understand back pain.

  • Are you in pain, even while laying in bed?
  • Does it take extra effort to even get out of bed?
  • Are there times you can't even stand straight until you've had a hot shower beating on your back?
  • Is pain stopping you from enjoying life?

Please call me if you are answering yes to even one of these questions.

Each session will include different massage techniques (depending on your injury), stretching and some will include heat
